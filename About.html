About Us

Natron Resources Inc. is an electrical engineering firm based in California. We have engineered projects across the United States.

Our expert staff provides comprehensive electrical engineering services for commercial and industrial projects as well as photovoltaic projects. We help startups and those new to the renewable energy industry get their products off the ground.

We routinely work on:

  • office and commercial buildings
  • manufacturing and laboratory facilities
  • sports fields, park and recreational facilities
  • hotels and motels
  • large and small housing projects
  • city street lighting projects
  • retail stores and dining facilities
  • schools and universities
  • government facilities
  • community centers.

    • We offer timely and cost-effective strategic, technical, analytic, design, and management services. It is important for our clients to remain competitive in the market, and we help them do so by reducing project timelines and cutting expenses on materials through value engineering.

    Our professional team is committed to delivering excellence and providing our clients with the highest level of service possible.
